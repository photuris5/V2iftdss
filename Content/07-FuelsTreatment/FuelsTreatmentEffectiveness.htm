Tutorial: Spatially Evaluating Fuels Treatment Effectiveness

This tutorial describes how to create a fuels treatment polygon and alter values (fuel model, canopy height, canopy base height, canopy bulk density, and canopy coverage) within that polygon to reflect a fuels treatment. After creating and configuring these polygons, you then run spatial fire behavior modules such as IFT-FlamMap, IFT-MTT, and IFT-RANDIG across the untreated and treated landscape to compare possible effects of treating the fuels on subsequent fire behavior, fire growth, and burn probability.

If you haven't already done so, read the tutorial on acquiring LANDFIRE data.
