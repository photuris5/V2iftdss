What can IFTDSS do for you?

IFTDSS is...

The Interagency Fuels Treatment Decision Support System (IFTDSS) is a web-based modeling framework. IFTDSS users can acquire, edit, or upload fuels data (Table 1), and run a variety of models to determine fire behavior, effects, vegetation changes, or run risk and hazard analysis (Table 2). IFTDSS has been developed for fuels treatment specialists by fuels treatment specialists to make fuels treatment planning and analysis more efficient and effective.

Choose your area

To use IFTDSS, you define an area of interest (IFTDSS refers to this as the project area), this is a large area which contains all of your stands, fire perimeters etc. When you specify your project, you will download, or upload an lcp file that area. Within the project area, you can create any number of modeling runs (IFTDSS refers to these as runs). When you perform a run, you will specify environmental variables, fuels data, and other information.

Choose and run your models

Models in IFTDSS are chosen by Workflow. Workflows are categorized by the results or process you are looking to model. Workflow categories in IFTDSS include:

  • Hazard Analysis
  • Risk Assessment
  • Fuels Treatment
  • Prescribed burn planning

For example, to model fire effects, you would click on Prescribed burn planning>Fire Effects and select between a number of effect
