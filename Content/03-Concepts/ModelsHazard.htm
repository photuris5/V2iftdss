Modules for Hazard Analysis

The module for burn probability from a random ignition (RANDIG) and the fire behavior module from FlamMap have been implemented in IFTDSS as IFT-FlamMap-RANDIG and IFT-FlamMap, respectively. These modules can be used to support hazard analysis by modeling potential fire behavior for an individual stand or across a landscape. These modules can also be used for prescribed burn planning and risk assessment.

To compute the burn probability from a random ignition and to compute potential fire behavior characteristics at a landscape level, you can upload a spatial dataset (i.e., LANDFIRE .lcp data) or define the spatial extent manually and retrieve data for the area specified.
