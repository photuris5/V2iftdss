FCCS Surface Fire Behavior Output Parameters

Name Units Brief Description

surface fire behavior
Flame Length Feet, meters Predicted flame length, measured as the distance between the flame length and the middle of the flame base.
Rate of Spread Feet/minute, meters/minute The rate at which the flaming front passes through surface fuels.
Reaction Intensity British thermal units/feet2/minute, kilowatts/meter2 The rate of the energy release per area within the flaming front.
Shrub Reaction Intensity British thermal units/feet2/minute, kilowatts/meter2 The rate of the energy release per area in the shrub strata within the flaming front.
Herbaceous Reaction Intensity British thermal units/feet2/minute, kilowatts/meter2 The rate of the energy release per area in the herbaceous strata within the flaming front.
Downed Wood Reaction Intensity British thermal units/feet2/minute, kilowatts/meter2 The rate of the energy release per area in the downed wood strata within the flaming front.
Litter, Lichen, and Moss Reaction Intensity British thermal units/feet2/minute, kilowatts/meter2 The rate of the energy release per area in the litter, lichen, and moss strata within the flaming front.
