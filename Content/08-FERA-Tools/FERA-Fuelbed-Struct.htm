Structural Classes

Structural classes are applicable to forested fuelbeds only. One structural class that best describes the number of canopy layers, relative size of trees, stage of understory development, and relative degree of stand closure is selected for each fuelbed. The structural classes are

  • Old-forest multistory
  • Old-forest single story
  • Stand initiation
  • Stem exclusion closed canopy
  • Stem exclusion open canopy
  • Understory re-initiation
  • Young-forest multistory
