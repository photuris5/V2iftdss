FEPS and Fire Information

As implemented in IFTDSS and BlueSky, FEPS calculates hourly consumption and emissions for a 24-hour period. For fire events that span multiple days, each day should be represented by a different run.

Fire Size

Enter the size of the fire for each 24-hour period.

Fire Shape

Enter either Oval for wildfires or Linear for prescribed burns.

Fuel Consumption

Fuel consumption values can be calculated in either Consume or IFT-FOFEM. Consume and FEPS can only be run together for a single fuelbed in the FFA. For burn units with multiple fuelbeds, total fuel consumption and emissions can be calculated in a Consume run and then manually entered in the FEPS pathway.

Four inputs are required:

  • Flaming consumption (fuel consumption during the flaming phase of combustion)
  • Smoldering consumption (fuel consumption during the smoldering phase of combustion)
  • Residual consumption (fuel consumption during the residual smoldering phase of combustion).
  • Duff fuel moisture (%)

Total Emissions

FEPS calculates hourly emissions for the following pollutant species:

  • PM2.5 (particulate matter < 2.5 microns in diameter)
  • PM10 (particulate matter < 10 microns in diameter)
  • CO (carbon monoxide)
  • CO2 (carbon dioxide)
  • CH4 (methane)
  • NOx (mono-nitrogen oxides)
  • NH3 (ammonia)
  • SO2 (sulfur dioxide)
  • VOC (volatile organic compounds)

Total emissions by pollutant species can be calculated using Consume and IFT-FOFEM. Consume outputs all pollutants allowed by FEPS. IFT-FOFEM calculates total emission for all pollutant species except for NH3 and VOC.
