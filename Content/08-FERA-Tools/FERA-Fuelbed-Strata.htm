Fuelbed Strata and Categories

FCCS fuelbeds use the following six strata and their associated categories.

Stratum Stratum Categories
Canopy Three categories:trees, snags, and ladder fuels
Shrub Records shrub inputs in primary and secondary shrub layers, in addition to needle drape.
Herbaceous Records nonwoody vegetation inputs (i.e., grasses, sedges, rushes, and forbs) in primary and secondary layers.
Downed wood Five categories: all downed and dead wood, sound wood, rotten wood, stumps, and piles.
Litter-lichen-moss records inputs on litter, lichen, and moss layers.
Ground fuels Three categories: duff, basal accumulations, and squirrel middens.
