Lofted and Drift PM2.5 Emissions

PM2.5 emissions are partitioned into two phases based on the calculated entrainment efficiency.

  • Lofted PM2.5 are those emissions entrained in the plume.
  • Drift emissions make up the remaining PM2.5 emissions.

Lofted PM2.5 is calculated as

L o f t e d P M 2.5 , i ⁢   =   E R P M 2.5 , i ⁢   E E f f , i

Drift PM2.5 is calculated as

D r i f t P M 2.5 , i ⁢   =   E R P M 2.5 , i ⁢   ( 1 − E E f f , i )
