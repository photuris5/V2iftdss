Southern Fuel Consumption Equations

Because woody fuels are sparse on southern pine sites, reliable fuel consumption equations were not available in the southern pine study for some woody fuel categories. This equation set uses the same consumption equations as the western woody equations for 1-hr and 10-hr sound wood and for large sound and rotten woody fuels >9 inch diameter.

Forest floor Cons Layer (tons/acre) = Loading PropRedFF

Where:
Loading (tons/acre) = preburn fuel load by LLM and ground fuel category.
PropRedFF = RedFF / total depth of LLM and duff layers
RedFF (inches) = (- 0.0061 DuffFM) + (0.6179 DepthFF)
DuffFM (%) = fuel moisture of duff, and
DepthFF (inches) = sum of input lichen, moss, litter, and duff depths.
