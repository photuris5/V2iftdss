FIRE INFORMATION

FUEL CONSUMPTION
Duff Moisture Percent The portion (%) of the duff that is water calculated on a dry weight basis
Flaming Consumption Tons/acre, Megagrams/hectare The amount of fuel consumed during the flame combustion phase of the fire.
Residual Consumption Tons/acre, Megagrams/hectare The amount of fuel consumed during the residual combustion phase of the fire. 
Smoldering Consumption Tons/acre, Megagrams/hectare The amount of fuel consumed during the smoldering combustion phase of the fire.
