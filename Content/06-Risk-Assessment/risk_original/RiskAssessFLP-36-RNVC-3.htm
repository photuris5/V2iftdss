Step 7b: Learn About the Five Relative Net Value Change Data Subsets

For example, if your data ranges from a numerical value of -5 to +10, the data subsets will be binned into the following data ranges:

  • -5 to -2
  • -2 to 1
  • 1 to 4
  • 4 to 7
  • 7 to 10
