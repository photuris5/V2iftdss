Analysis Considerations: Things You Need to Know

When reviewing and analyzing data, consider the following points:

  • The LANDFIRE data resolution is 30 x 30 m spatial resolution, which affects data interpretation.
  • The flame lengths probabilities approach assumes random ignitions across the area of interest; however, some areas have higher ignition potentials than others. This approach does not take this into account.
  • For example, human-caused ignitions often occur close to roads and houses, and natural ignitions caused by lightning occur at elevated areas.
