Acquiring FCCS fuelbed data to run IFT-Consume

From the Project Summary page, select Import Fuelbeds from LANDFIRE.

   Note   

You may also import LANDFIRE Fuelbeds, or upload a fuelbed dataset by clicking the Data tab and selecting Fuelbed data tab.

On the ‘Acquire Data from LANDFIRE’ page, choose a name for your dataset, and the version of LANDFIRE you want to use. For this example we are downloading LANDFIRE 2010 version 1.20. When finished, click Acquire.

Once the data have downloaded you will be returned to the project summary page.

Next you will scroll down to the Runs section of the project summary page.
