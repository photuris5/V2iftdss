IFT-FVS – Thin A Species Across a DBH Range – Input Parameters

Parameter Unit Description
Treatment Year Year Schedules a thinning from below to a basal area per acre target, or a trees per acre target. Only trees with dbh’s and heights within the user-defined ranges are considered for removal. Range: within the specified reporting period duration.
Smallest DBH to be considered for removal Inches The smallest DBH (greater than or equal to) to be considered for removal. Range from 1 to 200.
Largest DBH to be considered for removal Inches The largest DBH (less than) to be considered for removal. Range from 1 to 200.
Species to be removed in this cut NA Select a species to be thinned form the drop down list
Proportion of trees cut in specified species and DBH range NA The thinning will cut evenly across the DBH range specified. Range from 0.01 to 1.0.
