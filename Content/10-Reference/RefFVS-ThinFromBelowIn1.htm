IFT-FVS – Thin From Below – Input Parameters

Parameter Unit Description
Treatment Year Year Schedules a thinning from below to a basal area per acre target, or a trees per acre target. Only trees with dbh’s and heights within the user-defined ranges are considered for removal. Range: within the specified reporting period duration.
Stand density is in terms of: Drop-down list The stand's density will be represented by basal area or trees per area. In the next step, you will enter the residual stand density based on the chosen density parameter. Options: Trees per area (trees/acre), or Basal area (ft2/acre).
Residual Stand Density (within the specified tree size range) Feet2 per acres, Trees per acre The residual stand density (either basal area or trees per area) within the specified tree size range that is considered for removal. Range: 1 to 500 ft2/acre OR 1 to 1000 trees/acre.
Smallest DBH to be considered for removal Inches The smallest DBH (greater than or equal to) to be considered for removal. Range: 1 to 200.
Largest DBH to be considered for removal Inches The largest DBH (less than) to be considered for removal. Range: 1 to 200.
Proportion of small trees left NA The tree record with the smallest diameter within the specified range is considered for removal first, and the proportion of that tree record specified in here is removed. Range: 0.01 to 1.0.
Slash left Drop-down list The type of slash left at the site after the treatment. Options: No slash left; Whole trees left; Only branchwood left.
