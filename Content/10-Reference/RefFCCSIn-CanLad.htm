Surface Fire Behavior (FCCS) Inputs – Canopy Ladder Fuels

Name Units Brief Description
Is there vertical continuity between canopy and lower strata? Yes or No Either Yes or No. User must decide if certain fuels are plentiful enough to provide vertical continuity between tree crowns and lower strata. 
Ladder fuel type A drop-down list

 The drop-down list contains eight options for type of ladder fuel:
  • Arboreal lichens & moss
  • Climbing ferns & other epiphytes
  • Dead branches
  • Leaning snags
  • Profuse epicormic sprouting
  • Stringy or fuzzy bark
  • Tree regeneration
  • Vines / liana
