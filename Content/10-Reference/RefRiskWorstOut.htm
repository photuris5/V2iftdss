Output Parameters

Name Units Brief Description
Flame Length Feet, meters The distance from the ground at the leading edge of the flame to the tip of the flame.
Flame Length Class NA The flame lengths divided into low, medium, high, and very high user-defined classifications.
NVC Multiplier NA Intermediate value used to calculate Net Value Change based on the response function.
Overall Burn Probability Percentage The likelihood that the area represented by a pixel will burn given a random ignition across the area of interest (for a specific set of environmental conditions). Burn probability is not an estimate of the future likelihood of a wildfire and should not be confused with empirical wildfire occurrence probabilities.
Relative Net Value Change (Default) NA Relative net change in land value due to fire is assumed to be a function of fire behavior, and includes both positive and negative effects of fire.

Net Value Change (NVC) = Overall Burn Probability * NVC Multiplier

By default, the NVC output value range is binned by 20% for each of five categories.

  • Greatest Loss/Least Benefit
  • High Loss/Low Benefit
  • Average Loss/Benefit
  • Low Loss/High Benefit
  • Least Loss/Greatest Benefit

For more information, see Relative Net Value Change.
