Surface Fire Behavior (FCCS) Inputs – Environmental Parameters: Wind and Slope

On the Custom Fuel Moisture page, you can enter multiple custom moisture scenarios and save them with your fuelbed. You can select one custom moisture scenario for each run of the FCCS calculator.

Name Units Brief Description
Midflame windspeed Miles per hour The speed of wind measured at a flame’s midpoint. The midflame wind speed is used in the surface fire rate of spread calculations.
Slope Percentage The slope gradient is used in the surface fire rate of spread calculations.
