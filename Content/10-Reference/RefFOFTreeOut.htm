Tree Mortality Output Parameters

Name Units Brief Description
Average Mortality Percentage Average probability (percentage) of tree mortality.
Trees Killed An integer Total number of trees killed.
Average DBH of Killed Trees Inches, centimeters Average tree diameter (DBH) of fire-killed trees.
Average Mortality, DBH > 4 Percentage Percent mortality for trees having a DBH equal to or greater than 4 inches.
Total Trees per Fire An integer Total number of pre-fire live trees.
Basal Area: pre-Fire Square feet per acre / square meters per hectare The horizontal land area covered by live tree boles prior to burning.
Basal Area: post-Fire Square feet per acre / square meters per hectare The horizontal land area covered by live tree boles after burning.
Crown Cover: pre-Fire Percentage The horizontal land area covered by live tree crowns (foliage and branches) prior to burning, expressed as a percentage.
Crown Cover: post-Fire Percentage The horizontal land area covered by live tree crowns (foliage and branches) after burning, expressed as a percentage.
