Surface Fire Behavior Outputs – Palmetto-Gallberry

You can select one or more of these options.

Name Units Brief Description
Fuel bed depth Feet, meters Represents the depth of the surface fuel; this is a fuel model parameter. Fuel bed depth is sometimes called fuel bed bulk depth. A single fuel bed depth value must be used for a fuel model.
Palmetto-gallberry dead fine fuel load Tons per acre, tonne per hectare (Mg/ha) The fuel load of dead palmetto-gallberry (P-G) stems with 0- to 0.25-inch (0 to 0.64 cm) diameters. This value is calculated from P-G age of understory vegetation and P-G height of understory.
Palmetto-gallberry dead foliage fuel load Tons per acre, tonne per hectare (Mg/ha) The fuel load of dead palmetto-gallberry foliage that is still on the stem. This value is calculated from P-G age of understory vegetation and P-G palmetto coverage.
Palmetto-gallberry dead medium fuel load Tons per acre, tonne per hectare (Mg/ha) The fuel load of dead palmetto-gallberry stems with 0.25- to 1.0-inch (0.64 to 2.54 cm) diameters. This value is calculated from P-G age of understory vegetation and P-G palmetto coverage.
Palmetto-gallberry litter fuel load Tons per acre, tonne per hectare (Mg/ha) The load of the litter layer. This value is calculated from P-G age of understory vegetation and P-G overstory basal area.
Palmetto-gallberry live fine fuel load Tons per acre, tonne per hectare (Mg/ha) The fuel load of live palmetto-gallberry stems with 0- to 0.25-inch (0 to 0.64 cm) diameters. This value is calculated from P-G age of understory vegetation and P-G height of understory.
Palmetto-gallberry live foliage fuel load Tons per acre, tonne per hectare (Mg/ha) The load of live palmetto-gallberry foliage still on the stem. This value is calculated from P-G age of understory vegetation, P-G height of understory, and P-G palmetto coverage.
Palmetto-gallberry live medium fuel load Tons per acre, tonne per hectare (Mg/ha) The load of live palmetto-gallberry stems with 0.25- to 1.0-inch (0.64 to 2.54 cm) diameters. This value is calculated from P-G age of understory vegetation and P-G height of understory.
