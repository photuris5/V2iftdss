Surface Fire Behavior (FCCS) Inputs – Woody Fuels: All Downed and Dead Woody

The All Downed and Dead Woody category describes the depth and percent cover of continuous downed and dead sound and rotten woody fuels. Stumps and woody fuel accumulations are not included in this category.

Name Units Brief Description
Depth Feet Average depth of continuous sound and rotten downed woody fuels.

Min: minimum depth.
Mode: most common depth.
Max: maximum depth.
Total percent cover Percentage Total percent cover of continuous sound and rotten downed woody fuels.

Min: minimum percentage cover.
Mode: most common percentage cover.
Max: maximum percentage cover.
