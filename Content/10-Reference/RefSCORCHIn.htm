Crown Scorch Height Input Parameters

Name Units Brief Description
Air Temperature Degrees F, degrees C The ambient dry bulb temperature measured in the shade. This temperature is used to calculate scorch height and probability of ignition from a firebrand.
Midflame Wind Speed (upslope) Miles per hour, kilometers per hour The wind speed at midflame height above the fuel bed that affects surface fire spread. Midflame wind is often called eye-level wind.
