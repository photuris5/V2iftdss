Fireline Explosives Crews Production Rates

The following table compares the production rates of a 7-person fireline explosives crew and a 20-person hand crew over a 10-hour shift working in different fuel types. Table taken from National Wildfire Coordinating Group, 2004.

This information is used for the Resource Line Production Rate parameter. For other fireline production rate information used for this parameter, see Sustained Fireline Production Rates, Fireline Production Rates for Initial Action by Hand Crews, Fireline Production Rates for Initial Action by Engine Crews, Bulldozer Fireline Construction Rates, and Tractor-Plow Fireline Production Rates.
