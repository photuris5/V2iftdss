Surface Fire Size and Spread (IFT-size)

Surface fire size and growth characteristics for a point source fire can be estimated and used for developing a prescribed burn plan. The fire size module in IFTDSS produces the same results (which may differ slightly due to rounding) as the fire size modules in BehavePlus.

  • Input variables include wind, elapsed time, and rate of spread.
  • Outputs include fire size and growth characteristics.
