Element 3 (and Appendix C) - Complexity Analysis Summary

The purpose of the complexity rating process is to provide:

  • assignment of a complexity rating of high, moderate, or low to the prescribed fire
  • a relative ranking as to the overall complexity of a specific prescribed fire project for management and implementation personnel
  • a process that can be used to identify Prescribed Fire Plan elements or characteristics that may pose special problems or concerns

The Prescribed Fire Complexity Rating Guide was developed to assist personnel in determining a relative complexity of any single prescribed fire project.

The Summary Complexity Rating Rationale:

  • will clearly justify the summary rating for prescribed fire organization and Prescribed Fire Burn Boss level
  • must identify those risks from the Complexity Analysis (Appendix C) that are rated high and cannot be mitigated and will provide a discussion of the risks associated

(USDA and U.S. DOI, 2014, p. 25)
