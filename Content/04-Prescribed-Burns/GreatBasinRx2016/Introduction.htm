Great Basin Rx 301/341 Burn Plan Documentation in IFTDSS

The tools provided under the IFTDSS prescribed burn planning workflow aid fuels treatment planners and prescribed fire planners in assessing fire behavior and effects for varying fuel and environmental conditions.

IFTDSS also contains tools for preparing and generating a burn plan document that is based on the guidelines in the Interagency Prescribed Fire Planning and Implementation Procedures Reference Guide (USDA and U.S. DOI, 2014).

In this tutorial you will:

  • Set up a project in IFTDSS.
  • Select the burn plan template within IFTDSS
  • Populate the template based on information you gather from your Barber Park Prescribed Burn scenario throughout the week.

Citations and page numbers referenced in this exercise correspond to the 2014 Interagency Prescribed Fire Planning and Implementation Guide (U.S. Department of Agriculture and U.S. Department of the Interior)

   Note   

For a full tutorial on using the burn plan template within IFTDSS, see Tutorial C in the IFTDSS online help.
