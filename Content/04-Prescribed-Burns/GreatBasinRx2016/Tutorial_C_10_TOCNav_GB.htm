Table of Contents and Navigation

This is the Table of Contents for the burn plan. You can navigate from element to element by using any of the following:

  1. The bar at the top of the page
  2. The links located in the table of contents
  3. The Next button
   Note   

IFTDSS will allow users to generate the burn plan in Microsoft Word from any of the "Element" pages by clicking the Generate Burn Plan option at the bottom of the page. However, it is best to wait until information has been entered on all desired element pages before doing this, to avoid having multiple burn plan files in various states of completion. Clicking Next within IFTDSS will automatically save your progress
