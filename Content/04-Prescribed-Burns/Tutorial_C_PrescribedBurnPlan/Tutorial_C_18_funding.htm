Funding (Element 6)

Identify the funding source(s) and estimated cost(s) of the prescribed fire.

As shown here, some prescribed burns are conducted in phases; Element 6 can be itemized by phase if desired. (USDA and U.S. DOI, 2014, p. 28)

This is followed by an optional field in which expense tracking may be documented.
