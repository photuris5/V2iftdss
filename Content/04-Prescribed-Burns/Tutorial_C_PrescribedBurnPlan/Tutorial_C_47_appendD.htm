Appendix D: Agency-Specific Job Hazard Analysis

A job hazard analysis is a technique that focuses on job tasks as a way to identify hazards before they occur.

This analysis focuses on the relationship between the worker, the task, the tools, and the work environment.

Ideally, after you identify uncontrolled hazards, you will take steps to eliminate or reduce those hazards to an acceptable risk level.
