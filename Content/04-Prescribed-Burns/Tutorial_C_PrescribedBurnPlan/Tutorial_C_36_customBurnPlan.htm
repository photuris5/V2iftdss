Customizing the Burn Plan

After downloading the burn plan into a Microsoft Word document, you can easily customize the burn plan.

You can fill out Elements in the online burn plan template located within the IFTDSS website, and/or after generating the burn plan into a Word document.

For an example of a generated and customized Red Bull burn plan, navigate here in the IFTDSS online help: Prescribed Burn Planning > Tutorials > RedBull RX-341 Burn Plan.

   Note   

The online burn plan template has a 6,000-character limit per text box. If you need more characters, generate the burn plan into a Word document (which has no character limit), and continue editing.
