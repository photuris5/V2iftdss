Adding Map Details in Google Earth

Using the Layers tab in Google Earth, you can add such things as park and recreation areas, water body outlines, schools, airports, and roads to your map.

   Tip   

In Google Earth, click on the plus icon (or triangle icon if on a Mac) to the left of each layers to expand more detailed sublayers
