Selecting an Area of Interest

   Tip   

Give your runs descriptive names for future reference.

The next step is to select an area of interest within the project boundary. In this example, we selected the entire project boundary.

Selecting the entire project boundary is useful if you want to model fire behavior before defining a burn unit, and/or you want to model fire behavior for an entire project area.

After selecting an area of interest, choose Next.
