Things You Need to Know

Fire behavior values are calculated pixel-by-pixeland are simulated independently with respectto the surrounding pixels in the landscape input file.

Fire behavior in adjacent locations, roads, or hazardous fuel types in proximity to the area under assessment do not affect the burning potential of the pixel, or point location, being assessed.

The LANDFIRE data resolution is coarse (30 x 30 m spatial resolution),which affects data interpretation.
