Surface Fire Behavior (FCCS) Outputs – Potentials Report (Fire Potential Ratings 0–9

Name Units Brief Description
Available fuel potential Index between 0 and 9 Sum of fuel loadings in all combustion phases scaled to an index between 0 and 9.
Crown fire initiation potential Index between 0 and 9 Potential for fire to reach canopy layer.
Crown fire potential Index between 0 and 9 Weighted average of crown fire subpotentials.
Crown fire spreading potential Index between 0 and 9 Relative index of crown fire rate of spread.
Crown-to-crown transmissivity potential Index between 0 and 9 Potential for fire to carry through a canopy.
FCCS fire potential code Three-digit code Three-digit code representing the surface fire behavior, crown fire behavior, and available fuel potentials.
Flame available fuel potential Index between 0 and 9 Sum of fuel loadings available for the flaming phase of combustion (in units of 10 tons/acre). Multiply the index by 10 tons/acre to obtain the sum.
Flame length potential Index between 0 and 9 Proportional to fireline intensity or flame length.
Reaction potential Index between 0 and 9 Approximates the potential reaction intensity (energy released per unit area and time).
Residual available fuel potential Index between 0 and 9 Sum of fuel loadings available for the residual smoldering phase of combustion (in units of 10 tons/acre). Multiply the index by 10 tons/acre to obtain the sum.
Smoldering available fuel potential Index between 0 and 9 Sum of fuel loadings available for the smoldering phase of combustion (in units of 10 tons/acre). Multiply the index by 10 tons/acre to obtain the sum.
Spread potential Index between 0 and 9 Proportional to the no-wind rate of spread in surface fuel (distance per unit time).
Surface fire behavior potential Index between 0 and 9 Summary of surface fire behavior potential, calculated as the maximum of spread potential and flame length potential scaled to an index between 0 and 9.
