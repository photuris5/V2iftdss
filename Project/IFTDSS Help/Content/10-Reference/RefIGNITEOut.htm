Probability of Ignition Output Parameters

Name Units Brief Description
Probability of Ignition from a Firebrand Percentage The chance that a firebrand will cause an ignition. The number of firebrands, their size, and the fuel on which they land is generally unknown. Therefore, there is no specific interpretation of the probability of ignition of a fire. The calculation of probability of ignition is based on fuel shading from the sun, fine dead fuel moisture, and the air temperature.
Probability of Ignition from Lightning Percentage The chance that a lightning strike will ignite ground fuel of the type specified. This probability is calculated from fuel type, duff and litter depth, fuel moisture, and lightning strike type (negative, positive, or unknown).
