Safety Zone Size Input Parameters

Name Units Brief Description
Area per Heavy Equipment Feet2, meters2 The average area needed for each piece of equipment to be located within the safety zone. See the table below for some suggested values. For general planning when the equipment types are not known, use a mean area of 300 ft2.
Area per Person Feet2, meters2 The average area occupied by each person who is to be located within the safety zone. A reasonable figure is 50 ft2 (5 m2) per person, which is enough to allow shelter deployment if necessary.
Flame Length Feet, meters The flame length of a spreading surface fire within the flaming front is measured from midway in the active flaming combustion zone to the average tip of the flames.
Number of Heavy Equipment Digits The number of pieces of heavy equipment to be sheltered within the safety zone. Each piece is assumed to occupy the area specified by the Area per Heavy Equipment parameter.
Number of Personnel Digits The number of personnel to be located within the safety zone. This value includes all firefighters, overhead team members, and any other personnel to be protected in the safety zone.
