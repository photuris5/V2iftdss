IFT-FVS Mastication Input Parameters

Parameter Unit Description
Treatment Year Year Schedules a thinning from below to a basal area per acre target, or a trees per acre target. Only trees with dbh’s and heights within the user-defined ranges are considered for removal. Range: within the specified reporting period duration.
Smallest DBH to be considered for removal Inches The smallest DBH (greater than or equal to) to be masticated. Range: 1 to 200.
Largest DBH to be considered for removal Inches The largest DBH (less than) to be masticated. Range: 1 to 200.
Species to be masticated Drop-down list Select a species to be masticated from the drop-down list.
Size of masticated fuel Drop-down list The after-treatment chip size of the masticated trees. Options: 1-hr woody fuel (0 to 0.25 in); 10-hr woody fuel (.25 to 1 in); 100-hr woody fuel (1 to 3 in); 1000-hr woody fuel (3 to 6 in).
Proportion of surface fuel composed of masticated pieces NA The proportion of the surface fuel composed of masticated pieces should be estimated by the proportion of the after-treatment surface fuel that is from masticated trees. For example, this should be set to 1.0 if there is no surface fuel present before treatment. If you have 5 tons/ac of surface fuel initially and chip 5 tons/ac, this should be set to 0.5. Range: 0.01 to 1.0.
