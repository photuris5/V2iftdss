Surface Fire Behavior (FCCS) Inputs – Canopy Trees

There are four subcategories of canopy trees:

  1. Total canopy cover: Total percentage cover by crown projection of all tree layers.
  2. Overstory trees: Uppermost canopy layer, including emergent, dominant, and codominant trees.
  3. Midstory trees: Intermediate canopy layer, including trees below the overstory and above the understory layer.
  4. Understory trees: Lowermost canopy layer, including seedlings, saplings, and other small trees.
