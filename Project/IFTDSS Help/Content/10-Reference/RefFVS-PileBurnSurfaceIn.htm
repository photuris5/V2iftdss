IFT-FVS Pile Burn Surface Input Parameters

Parameter Unit Description
Percent of fuels within stand area piled Percent The percentage of the fuel from the affected area that is concentrated in the treated area.  80% is suggested for a pile burn and 60% is suggested for a jackpot burn.

Range: 0 to 100.
Percent of stand area covered by piles Percent The percentage of the affected area into which the fuel is concentrated (the area which will be treated). 10% is suggested for a pile burn and 30% is suggested for a jackpot burn.

Range: 0 to 100.
Percent of stand area treated Percent The percentage of the stand's area affected by the pile burn treatment. 70% is suggested for pile burns and 100% is suggested for a jackpot burn.

Range: 0 to 100.
Treatment Year Year Schedules a pile burn of surface fuel. In addition, you can simulate tree mortality from this burning.

Range: within the specified reporting period duration.
Tree mortality Percent The percentage mortality of trees in the stand caused by the pile burn treatment.

Range: 0 to 100.
