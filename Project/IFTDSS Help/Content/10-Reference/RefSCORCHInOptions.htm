Crown Scorch Height Input Options

Name Units Brief Description
Fireline Intensity Btus per feet per second, kilowatts per meter The heat energy release per unit of time from a one-foot (one-meter) wide section of the fuel bed extending from the front to the rear of the flaming zone. Fireline intensity is a function of rate of spread and heat per unit area, and is directly related to flame length.
Flame Length Feet, meters The flame length of a spreading surface fire within the flaming front is measured from midway in the active flaming combustion zone to the average tip of the flames.
