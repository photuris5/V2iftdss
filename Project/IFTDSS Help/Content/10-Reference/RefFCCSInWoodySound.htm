Surface Fire Behavior (FCCS) Inputs – Woody Fuels: Sound Wood

The Sound Wood category displays loading (tons/acre) data for a range of size classes of downed sound woody debris. Species designations and relative cover apply to all sound wood size classes in two groupings: 0-3 inches diameter and > 3 inches diameter.
