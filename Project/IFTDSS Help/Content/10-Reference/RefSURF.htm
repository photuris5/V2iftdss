Surface Fire Behavior (IFT-surface)

Surface fire behavior can be calculated for individual stands. The fire behavior module in IFTDSS produces the same results (which may differ slightly due to rounding) as the fire behavior module in BehavePlus.

  • Input variables include fuel model type, moisture, wind, direction, and slope.
  • The module provides fire behavior characteristics and wind, slope, and fuel characteristics.
