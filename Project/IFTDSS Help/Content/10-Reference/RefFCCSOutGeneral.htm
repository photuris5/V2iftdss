Surface Fire Behavior (FCCS) Outputs – General

The General Report includes the basic fuel characteristics for the following categories:

  • All canopy categories (total canopy, trees, total snags, and ladder fuels)
  • Ground fuels categories (total ground fuels, upper duff, lower duff, and basal accumulations)
  • Litter, lichen, and moss categories (litter and total litter, lichen, and moss)
  • Shrubs (total shrubs)
  • Nonwoody fuels (total nonwoody fuels)
  • Woody fuels categories (total woody fuels, sound wood results, rotten wood results, and stumps)
