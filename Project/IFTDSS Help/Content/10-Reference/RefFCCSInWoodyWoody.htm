Surface Fire Behavior (FCCS) Inputs – Woody Fuels: Woody Fuel Accumulation

There are three subcategories of woody fuel accumulation:

  1. Jackpots: Natural accumulation and concentration of woody debris.
  2. Piles: Woody debris that is formed into a pile for future burning.
  3. Windrows: Woody debris that is piled and left following timber harvesting.
