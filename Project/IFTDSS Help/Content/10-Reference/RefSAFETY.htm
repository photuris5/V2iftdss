Safety Zone Size (IFT-safety)

You can calculate safety zone sizes (which includes separation distance, zone size, and zone radius) and use that information when developing a prescribed burn plan. The safety zone module implemented in IFTDSS produces the same results (which may differ slightly due to rounding) as the safety zone module in BehavePlus.

  • Input variables include flame length, number of personnel and heavy equipment, and area occupied by each person and piece of heavy equipment within the safety zone.
  • Output variables include safety zone size, safety zone radius, and safety zone separation distance (Butler and Cohen, 1996; 1998a; 1998b).
