IFT-FVS – Thin From Below – Input Parameters

Parameter Unit Description
Treatment Year Year Schedules a pile burn of surface fuel. In addition, you can simulate tree mortality from this burning. Range: within the specified reporting period duration.
Percent of stand area treated Percent The percent of the stand's area affected by the pile burn treatment. 70% is suggested for pile burns and 100% is suggested for a jackpot burn. Range: 0 to 100.
Percent of fuels within stand area piled Percent The percent of the fuel from the affected area that is concentrated in the treated area. 80% is suggested for a pile burn and 60% is suggested for a jackpot burn. Range: 0 to 100.
Percent of stand area covered by piles Percent The percent of the affected area into which the fuel is concentrated (the area which will be treated). 10% is suggested for a pile burn and 30% is suggested for a jackpot burn. Range: 0 to 100.
Tree mortality Percent The percent mortality of trees in the stand caused by the pile burn treatment. Range: 0 to 100.
