BehavePlus SAFETY Compared to IFT-safety

Using IFT-safety, users can input all the parameters provided in BehavePlus and obtain the same output variables; however, the BehavePlus option of auto-populating the safety module calculation inputs from surface or crown fire behavior simulations does not exist in IFTDSS.

In BehavePlus, SAFETY can be coupled with SURFACE; then the worst-case scenario for flame length from SURFACE is directly entered into SAFETY.

In IFTDSS, the user runs the fire behavior modules of interest, records the fire behavior outputs, and then hand-enters these outputs into the IFT-safety modules.
