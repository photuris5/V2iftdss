Introduction

In this tutorial, we focus on the flame length probabilities approach. This method uses the response functions developed by Calkin et. al. (2010a) and modeled flame length burn probabilities from the IFT-FlamMap-RANDIG burn probability simulator to estimate the likelihood of the area represented by the pixel burning, and the potential consequences if the area represented by the pixel is burned by a backing fire, a flanking fire, or a head fire.
