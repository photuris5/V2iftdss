FFA Models and Modules

As mentioned in the overview of modules in IFTDSS, the modules in IFTDSS can be arranged by the type of tool they are. The following models and modules all fall under the category of Fire and Fuels Application (FFA) tools. Outputs vary depending on which model or module you use.

Category Models/Modules Function
FCCS fuel characteristics
  • All fuel characteristics
  • Fuel loading
  • Total carbon
 These three provide a variety of output variables based on a selected FCCS fuelbed.
FCCS fire potentials
  • All fire and fuel potentials
  • Surface fire behavior potential
  • Crown fire potential
  • Available fuel potential
 These four categories provide output variables based on a selected FCCS fuelbed. The surface fire behavior potential model also uses a moisture scenario as input.
FCCS fire behavior Surface fire behavior This module provides output variables based on a selected FCCS fuelbed, a moisture scenario, slope, and wind conditions.
Consumption and emissions
  • Fuel consumption
  • Heat release
  • Pollutant emissions
  • Total fuel consumption, emissions, and heat release
 These four provide output variables based on a selected FCCS fuelbed, 1000-hr fuel and duff moisture, percentage canopy loading consumed, and percentage of shrub blackened.
