LANDFIRE 2010

The LANDFIRE 2010 version 1.2.0 data were delivered in 2013 and improve upon the LANDFIRE Refresh 2008 1.1.0b data currently available.

The LANDFIRE 2010 update includes:

  • Disturbance 1999 - 2010, landscape change from natural disturbances (e.g., wildland fires) and management activities. Disturbance and ecological change occurring 2011 or later will not be reflected in these data.
  • Urban expansion according to National Land Cover Database 2006 (NLCD06).
  • Agricultural refinement using National Agricultural Statistics Service's Cropland Data Layer 2011 (CDL).
  • Wetlands refinement using NLCD06 and National Wetlands Inventory (NWI).

Users will notice an increase in the amount of rock represented as a fuel model 99, especially in higher elevation areas compared to LANDFIRE Refresh 2008.

Canopy fuels (canopy base height, canopy bulk density, stand height and canopy cover) should be similar to LF Refresh 2008, but will reflect changes in areas with disturbance 2008 to 2010.
