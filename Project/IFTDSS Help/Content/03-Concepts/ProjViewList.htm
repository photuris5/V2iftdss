Viewing and Managing Your Projects

From the Home page or from the Projects tab at the top of the page, you can access the Active Projects page, which lists all the projects that you have created.

On this page, you can edit, archive, or delete an existing project, or you can create a new project.

When you archive a project, the project is moved to an archive list. From the archived projects list, you can retrieve and reactivate a project.

All projects are saved within IFTDSS until you delete them.
