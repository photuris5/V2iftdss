Finding Other Users

You can search for other users by accessing the Collaborate tab or choosing the Find Other Users link on the navigation page.

On the Collaborate tab, there is a List Users link.

Clicking that link takes you to an alphabetical list of everyone who has an IFTDSS account. By default, account holders are listed ten people at a time. You can change that by clicking the Show drop-down list and choosing a different number (or All).

You can also search for users. The Name, Organization, and Location fields are searched. The search is live; as you type your text, search results are displayed and updated instantly. Clicking a user's name takes you to that person's profile.
