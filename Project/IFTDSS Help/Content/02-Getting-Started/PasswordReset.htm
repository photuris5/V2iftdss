Resetting your Password

To reset your password you can either click on the down arrow on the right side of the Go button, or follow the links for resetting your password that will appear when an incorrect username or password is entered.

Once the Password Reset screen opens, enter your username and a recovery link will be sent to the email listed in your User Profile.

Follow the link enclosed in the IFTDSS Password Reset email to reset your password.

 

You will be prompted to enter a new password, then enter it a second time for verification, and submit your new password.

Once your new password is submitted you will be redirected to a Sign In page where you may now access IFTDSS using your new password.
