Available Fuel Potential Output Parameters

Available fuel potential estimates the oven-dry, combustible biomass in the flaming, smoldering, and residual smoldering phases of combustion. The index is expressed in units of 10 tons/acre and estimates total fuel loading of all fuel elements within a set depth from the surface of the fuel component.

  • Flame available fuel (FA). The sum of mass (tons/ac/10) within one-half inch of the surface of the fuel element, including the mass of thermally thin fuel elements in the canopy, surface fuels, and ground fuels stratum.
  • Smoldering available fuel (SA)(0-10). Mass between ½ and 2 inches of a fuel surface.
  • Residual available fuel (RA)(0-10). Mass between 2 and 4 inches of a fuel surface.
Name Units Brief Description
Available Fuel Potential NA The sum of fuel loadings in all combustion phases scaled to an index between 0-9.  AFP = min(9, sum(FA,SA,RA))
Flame Available Fuel Potential 10 tons/acre The sum of fuel loadings available for the flaming phase of combustion.  FA = FA/10
Residual Available Fuel Potential 10 tons/acre The sum of fuel loadings available for the residual smoldering phase of combustion.  RA = RA/10
Smoldering Available Fuel Potential 10 tons/acre The sum of fuel loadings available for the smoldering phase of combustion.  SA = SA/10
