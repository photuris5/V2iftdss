Vegetation Forms

FCCS fuelbeds are classified as being one of the following seven vegetation forms:

  • Broadleaf forest.
  • Conifer forest.
  • Grassland
  • Mixed forest. Mixed forests are forests in which evergreen and deciduous species each contribute 25 to 75 percent of total tree cover in both the overstory and midstory strata.
  • Savanna.
  • Shrubland.
  • Slash.
