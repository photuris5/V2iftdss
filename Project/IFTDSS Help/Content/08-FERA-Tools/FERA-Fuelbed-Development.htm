FCCS Fuelbed Overview

FCCS contains over 280 fuelbeds that you can select and modify for your specific application. FCCS fuelbeds were developed by the Fire and Environmental Applications Team (FERA) to represent fuel characteristics throughout the United States.

The fuelbeds contain data from regional workshops, published literature, USDA Forest Service photo series, general technical reports and research papers, other government literature and large databases (e.g., National Park Service and Forest Inventory and Analysis), master's and Ph.D. theses, white papers, field data and other unpublished data, and expert opinion. Each fuelbed is assigned a data quality ranking from 1 (inputs based on expert opinion) to 5 (all inputs based on published literature).
