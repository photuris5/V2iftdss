Estimations for Uncured 1000-Hour Woody Fuel Moisture

The 1000-hour fuels are uncured if (1) snow-off occurred within the past three months, or (2) the fuelbed was harvested within the past few months. Refer to this table for an uncured fuel moisture that best represents your burn.

Condition Recommended Uncured 1000-hr Fuel Moisture
Snow-off occurred within the last three months 120%
Harvest occurred within the last month 120%
Harvest occurred one to two months ago 80%
Harvest occurred two to three months ago 67%
Harvest occurred over three months ago 60%
