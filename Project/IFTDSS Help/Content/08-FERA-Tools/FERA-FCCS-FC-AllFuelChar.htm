All Fuel Characteristics Output Parameters

The FCCS fuel characteristics output parameters are in the following table. Below this table is another describing the exported output parameters.

Name Units Brief Description

total fuel loading
Fuel Strata Tons/acre, megagram/hectare Mass per unit area of fuel strata.

percent cover
Fuel Strata Percent Total percent cover of fuel strata.

height and depth
Fuel Strata(includes Canopy, Shrubs, and Herbaceous) Feet, meters The average distance from the bottom to the top of the fuel strata layer.  The term height is used for the canopy, shrub, and herbaceous strata.
Fuel Strata(includes Downed Wood, Litter, Lichen, and Moss, and Total Duff) Inch, centimeter The average distance from the bottom to the top of the fuel stratalayer.  The term "depth" is used for the downed wood, litter, lichen, and moss, and total duff strata.

density
Tree and snag subcategories Count/acre, count/hectare Number per acre of thetree and snag subcategories.

tree height to live crown
Tree subcategory Feet, meters Height to live crown of the tree subcategory.

diameter at breast height
Tree and snag subcategories Inch, centimeter Diameter at breast height of the tree and snag subcategories.
