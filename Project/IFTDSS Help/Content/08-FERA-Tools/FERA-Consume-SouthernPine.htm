Southern Pine Consumption Study

In the FERA southern pine studies, a total of 18 sites were sampled in longleaf and loblolly pine forests in the southeastern United States.Sampled stands represent units that are under a frequent prescribed burn rotation.Sites are in the Atlantic Coastal Plain. Consumption estimates were taken for downed woody fuel and forest floor reduction following the methods described under Western Fuel Consumption: Ponderosa Pine Studies. Most burns were conducted in the dormant season. The following table shows the prescribed burn units (n=60) in the southern pine consumption study.
